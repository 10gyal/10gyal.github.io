About Me

I am a senior at Seoul National University majoring in Computer Science and Engineering. I also work as an NLP engineer at Rezi.

Interests

I enjoy solving a range of NLP problems. I have hands on experience starting from curating datasets, tokenization and embedding schemes, all the way to finetuning opensource LLMs such as Mixtral with QLoRA. Currently, I am working on customer service dialogue generation in Korean.

Projects

  • Skill Extraction is an ensemble of models for extracting different classes of skills from a job description. I built it during my internship at rezi.ai. This also served as my graduation thesis
  • exp_key_relevance is a combination of token and sentence level vector similarity comparisons for finding the most relevant experience in a resume given a keyword.
  • Built a recommendation system for ranking resumes based on keywords. The system was a hybrid of tradtional keyword matching and vector based (semantic) searches.
  • Worked on a capstone project in collaboration with KAIST and SherpaSpace to predict infections in pineapples in a farm in Malaysia. Delivered two time series models, CNN & LSTM, that predicted infections with 97% & 98% accuracies respectively.
